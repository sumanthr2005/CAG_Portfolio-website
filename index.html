Clean and Green Energy Portfolio - 21EEO307T

Welcome to my portfolio website created as part of the course CLEAN AND GREEN ENERGY (21EEO307T) under the guidance of Dr.J.Divya Navamani. This website presents three design-based activities focused on sustainable and renewable energy systems, developed to demonstrate the practical application of clean energy technologies. Each activity includes a complete system architecture, descriptive explanation, and insights into the energy conversion process, output utilization, and real-world significance.

As the global demand for energy continues to rise, clean energy sources like tidal, biomass, and ocean thermal energy provide sustainable alternatives to fossil fuels. The primary objective of this portfolio is to explore the working principles, design considerations, and benefits of these renewable energy systems while emphasizing environmental responsibility and long-term feasibility.

  • Activity 2: Biomass-Based Biogas Production System
  • Activity 3: Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion (OTEC) System

Each section contains a detailed block diagram, system explanation, and critical aspects such as advantages, disadvantages, site selection, real-world applications and challening questions with its answers. The goal is to provide a clear understanding of how renewable energy systems function and how they can be integrated to create a sustainable future for our planet.
